Samsung Galaxy S9

Samsung Galaxy S9 smartphone was launched in March 2018. The phone comes with a 5.80-inch touchscreen display with a resolution of 1440 pixels by 2960 pixels at a PPI of 568 pixels per inch.
The Samsung Galaxy S9 is powered by octa-core (4x2.7GHz + 4x1.7GHz) processor and it comes with 4GB of RAM.
The phone packs 64GB of internal storage that can be expanded up to 400GB via a microSD card.
